Meet Fi

Hi, I'm Fi Fabrizi.

I'm a 26-year-old lifelong sports fanatic who believes sports are about so much more than the final score.

Ever since I can remember, sports have been a huge part of my life. My husband and I love attending games, watching just about anything that's on, and keeping up with the biggest stories across the sports world.

While soccer and football are my biggest passions, I genuinely love following all sports. If there's a championship on, a rivalry game happening, or an underdog making a run, I'm probably watching.

I created Fi's Playbook because I realized sports can feel intimidating if you didn't grow up following them. My goal is to make sports fun, easy to understand, and something everyone can enjoy—especially for those who just want to know enough to join the conversation without needing ESPN on in the background 24/7.

Whether you're here to keep up with the biggest stories, learn something new, or finally understand what everyone at the office is talking about on Monday morning, you're in the right place.
