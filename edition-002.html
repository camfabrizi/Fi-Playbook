🏆 What happened this week

The World Cup final four is set.

  • Spain survived Belgium 2–1. Mikel Merino's late winner sent Spain to its second World Cup semifinal.
  • France beat Morocco 2–0. Kylian Mbappé scored after an earlier penalty miss, and France moved on.
  • England ended Norway's Cinderella run 2–1 after extra time. Jude Bellingham scored twice to book England's semifinal place.
  • Argentina survived another scare, beating Switzerland 3–1 after extra time. Julián Álvarez broke the tie before Lautaro Martínez sealed it.

“Late goals, missed penalties, VAR drama, and plenty of stress.”
