⚽ What happened this week

Spain are World Cup champions.

Spain defeated Argentina 1–0 to lift the World Cup trophy, and honestly—they earned it. From the opening whistle, they looked more composed, more patient, and tactically one step ahead.

Things got even tougher for Argentina when Enzo Fernández was shown a red card, forcing them to finish the match with 10 players.

The game stayed scoreless through regulation, but Ferran Torres buried an absolute beauty in extra time to break the deadlock. Argentina pushed for an equalizer, but Spain's defense slammed the door shut.

🛋️ Fi's couch report

Spain won because they had a plan.

“Sometimes the prettiest soccer isn't about flashy tricks.”

Spain stayed patient, trusted its style, and never looked rattled—even before Argentina went down to 10 players.

Sometimes beautiful soccer is about making the other team chase shadows for 120 minutes.

🤯 Sports fun fact

FIFA keeps the original World Cup trophy. Champions receive an identical gold-plated replica to take home.
