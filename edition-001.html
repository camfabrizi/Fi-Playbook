🎟️ Fi's field report

Inside Argentina–Egypt

“I've never experienced anything like it.”

The chants echoed through the entire stadium for 90-plus minutes. Every Messi touch felt like the whole crowd stood up at once, and when Argentina completed the comeback, it was absolute chaos.

People were hugging strangers, waving flags, and singing at the top of their lungs. It genuinely felt like the entire stadium became one giant family.

10/10 would lose my voice again.
